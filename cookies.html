Procedures

  1. Using a spatula, mix butter with white and brown sugar. Mix until light and smooth. (10 mins)
  2. Add All Purpose Cream, then mix in eggs and vanilla extract until well combined. (3 mins)
  3. Sift flour, baking soda, and salt. Add to cream mixture and mix until just combined. Do not overmix. Mix in chocolate chips. (5 mins)
  4. Refrigerate dough for about 2 hours. After chilling, mold into golf-ball size portions and chill for another 10 mins. (20 mins)

EGG PIE

A Filipino favorite with a creamy custard filling and buttery crust. Baked until golden brown and slightly caramelized on top. A warm, comforting dessert loved by many

PROCEDURES

    For the crust:

  1. Whisk together flour, salt, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add cubed butter and mix until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Add egg yolk, vanilla, and ice water (save the egg white for later).
  4. Mix until a dough forms; knead lightly until smooth.
  5. Cover the dough and refrigerate for 1 hour.
  6. Roll out the chilled dough on a floured surface (about 1/8" thick).
  7. Place into a 9-inch pie dish, trim, and crimp edges neatly.
  8. Refrigerate the crust while preparing the filling.

  10. Warm the evaporated milk in a saucepan until just lukewarm.
  11. In a bowl, beat the eggs and gradually add sugar while whisking.
  12. Stir in the warmed milk and vanilla extract until smooth.
  13. Strain the mixture to remove bubbles or lumps (optional).
  14. Pour the filling into the prepared crust.
  15. Beat the reserved egg white until frothy and gently spoon it over the top.

  17. Cool completely before slicing and serving.
